# Shipping Policy

At Animeniacs, we aim to make your shopping experience as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Here's all you need to know about our shipping process.

## Production Time (Lead Time)

1. **Preparing Your Order:** Once you place an order, it typically takes 2-4 days to print your wall art.
2. **Quality Check & Shipping:** After your wall art or LightBox is ready, it undergoes a final quality check. Once the label is printed, you will receive an email with your tracking number to monitor your order's journey.

Note: If LightBoxes are out of stock, shipping times will vary until we restock. We will ship it as soon as possible once back in stock.

## Shipping Rates

- **United States:** $15 – $20 USD
- **International:** $25 – $70 USD

## Shipping Time

- **United States:** FEDEX Ground, 3-6 business days.
- **Worldwide:** FEDEX Express, 7-10 business days.

Note: Shipping times may be affected by public holidays, destination countries, and couriers.

## Wrong Address Policy

If you accidentally entered the wrong shipping address, don't worry—we've got you covered.

Please note that as a small business, each time we reship or reprint an order, it incurs additional costs. Therefore, we ask for your understanding and cooperation with the following policy:

- If an error was made in the shipping address, we will send you an invoice to cover the reshipping cost.
- If preferred, you can opt for a refund, minus the shipping cost and a 20% penalty, as we cannot restock the item.

We understand mistakes happen and are here to help. Just double-check your address when placing an order, and if there's an issue, let us know ASAP at Biz@Animeniacs.Shop.

## Additional Notes

Shipping charges for accessories vary based on the product and quantity purchased. Detailed shipping charges will be displayed at checkout.

For any further questions or information, please contact us at Biz@Animeniacs.Shop.

Thank you for choosing Animeniacs! We are excited to serve you and ensure your order reaches you promptly and safely.
