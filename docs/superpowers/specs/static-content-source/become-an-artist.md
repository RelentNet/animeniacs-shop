# Become an Artist with Animeniacs

Are you a talented artist looking to share your work with a passionate global community of anime, gaming, and fandom enthusiasts? Animeniacs partners with creators to bring their art to a worldwide audience as premium acrylic wall art, vinyl decals, and more.

## What We Offer

- **Global Exposure:** Your art reaches fans around the world through our store, conventions, and partnerships.
- **Commission on Every Sale:** Earn a commission every time one of your pieces is sold. Get paid through our affiliate platform.
- **No Inventory Risk:** We handle production, fulfillment, and customer service. You focus on creating.
- **Your Own Artist Page:** Every featured artist gets a dedicated profile page on animeniacs.shop with your bio, social links, and a gallery of all your work.

## How It Works

1. **Apply:** Send us your portfolio and a brief intro at Biz@Animeniacs.Shop.
2. **Review:** Our team reviews submissions for fit with our community and quality standards.
3. **Onboard:** Once approved, you sign our standard artist agreement and we set up your profile.
4. **Submit Designs:** Upload your designs through our partner portal. We handle the rest.
5. **Earn:** Get paid monthly for every sale your work generates.

## How to Get Started

Send us an email at Biz@Animeniacs.Shop with:

- A link to your portfolio (Instagram, ArtStation, personal site, etc.)
- A short bio about you and your art
- Any specific franchises, IPs, or themes you specialize in

We review every submission personally and reply within 1-2 weeks.

We can't wait to see what you create.
