# How to Display Your Art

## Step-by-Step Hanging Guide

To make hanging your new art piece effortless, we provide Large Hanging Command Strips with every online order. If you purchase your artwork at one of our events or fairs, you can get these strips for just $5.

## Where to Find Command Strips

In case you need more strips, they are readily available at major retail stores such as Walmart, CVS, Walgreens, and Target. They are also accessible online through Amazon.

## Using the Command Strips

1. **Prepare Your Wall and Art**: Clean the wall surface and the back of your art piece with rubbing alcohol. Avoid using household cleaners.
2. **Apply the Strips**: Attach the Command Strips to the back of your art, following the package instructions.
3. **Position Your Art**: Press the strips firmly against the wall for 30 seconds.
4. **Secure and Adjust**: Hold the art in place for a minute to ensure it's firmly attached. Adjust as necessary.
