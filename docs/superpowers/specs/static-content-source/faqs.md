If you accidentally entered the wrong shipping address, please understand that we are a small business and each re-shipment incurs additional costs. Alternatively, you can opt for a refund, minus the shipping cost and a 20% penalty, as we cannot restock the item. Double-check your address when ordering and contact us ASAP if there's an issue.
