# Collaborate with Animeniacs

At Animeniacs, we love partnering with content creators, brands, and businesses to create exciting and mutually beneficial collaborations. Here's how you can get involved:

## Partnership Opportunities

### 1. Affiliate Marketing

- Promote Animeniacs products and earn commissions. We provide you with a unique 10% off promo code to share with your audience. For every purchase made using your code, you'll receive 10% of the sale.
- Requirements: A minimum of 5 purchases generated through your code.

### 2. Bulk Ordering

- If you're interested in making a large purchase, use the promo code "Bulk" when ordering 15 or more items to get a 20% discount. This is perfect for businesses, events, or large groups.
- You can also upload your own designs using our "Upload Any Image" feature to create custom artwork.

### 3. Business to Business Inquiries

- We collaborate with various businesses for unique projects. If you're looking to create a custom product or have a special project in mind, reach out to us for more details.

## Why Collaborate with Us?

- **Free Artwork and Discount Codes:** As part of our collaboration, we offer free artwork and exclusive discount codes for your audience.
- **Shout Outs and Promotions:** Gain exposure through our platform as we promote your content to our audience.
- **Support for Artists:** Every purchase supports the artist who created the design, fostering a community of creativity and support.

## How to Get Started

Interested in collaborating with Animeniacs? Send us an email at Biz@Animeniacs.Shop with your proposal or any questions you might have. We look forward to creating something amazing together!
