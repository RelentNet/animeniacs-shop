# About Us

Welcome to Animeniacs!

At Animeniacs, our vision is to empower video gamers, trading card gamers, and fandom lovers with custom and limited product releases. Based in New Orleans, LA, we live by the motto "laissez le bon temps rouler" – let the good times roll!
